Privacy Policy

At AGIAL School, we value the privacy and security of our students, parents, and staff. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

We only collect data that helps us provide educational services and maintain effective communication between the school and families. Any personal information shared with us is kept confidential and used solely for official school purposes.

We do not share or sell your data to any third party. Access to personal information is limited to authorized staff members only, in compliance with data protection standards.
